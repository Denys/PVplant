### EZ1 Microinverter Series

Wi-Fi Version for DIY

The Wi-Fi version of EZ1 series are APsystems 3rd generation of dual microinverters, they are dedicated designed for balcony and DIY systems, EZ1 series microinverters have 2 input channels with independent MPPT and high input current and output power to adapt to today’s larger power module.

Users could directly connect to the EZ1 series with their cell phones through Bluetooth and get the real-time data of the solar systems. Besides direct connection, EZ1 series could also connect to a router through Wi-Fi and send data to cloud servers for remote monitoring.

Through an AC extension cable provided by APsystems, EZ1 series could be plugged into a socket and start output energy, truly easy and convenient grid connection.

![](https://global.apsystems.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/diagram.jpg)

### Key Features

• One microinverter connects to two modules

• Two input channels with independent MPPT

• High input current to adapt to large modules

• Maximum reliability, IP67

• Built in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth

• Safety protection relay integrated

• Dedicated for balcony and DIY systems

• Max output power reaching 600/799 or 960VA
