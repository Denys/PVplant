# HMS-300&350&400&450&500-1T

Hoymiles’s new photovoltaic microinverter HMS-500-1T series is connected to one panel and is flexible for various applications.

All of the Hoymiles HMS-500-1T series are equipped with reactive power control and can meet the requirements of EN 50549-1:2019, VDE-AR-N 4105:2018, VFR2019, etc.

HMS-500-1T series with the new Sub-1G wireless solution enables more stable communication under various environmental conditions.
