# Microinverter 6-in-1

2400 W - 3300 W

TSUN Microinverter 6-in-1 is a compact and high-efficiency microinverter designed to connect with six solar panels. Engineered with state-of-the-art topology, a modular build, and full aluminum die-casting for outstanding heat dissipation and reliability, it ensures safe and reliable energy conversion at the module level. Delivering up to 3300 W output power, it supports 6 PV inputs, optimized by MPPT technology, and provides module-level monitoring for enhanced performance. With a low startup voltage of 22 V and multiple communication options for easy monitoring, it adapts flexibly to different setups. Fully encapsulated to IP67, the microinverter offers long-lasting durability with a 25-year lifespan, while supporting three different wiring methods to suit diverse installation scenarios.
