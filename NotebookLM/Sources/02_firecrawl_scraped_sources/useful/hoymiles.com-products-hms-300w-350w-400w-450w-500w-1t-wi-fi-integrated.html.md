# HMS-300&350&400&450&500W-1T

Hoymiles HMS-500W series microinverters are specifically designed for mini solar systems in open spaces like balconies, Hoymiles HMS-500W series microinverters feature integrated industrial+grade Wi-Fi module. Their output power reaches up to 500 VA.

With the user-friendly HMS Plug and Play Cable, installation is effortless for users of all levels. Users can connect the microinverter directly to a socket—no complex wiring is required.

Additionally, system monitoring is simplified for the HMS-500W series with two convenient options: real-time monitoring via Direct Connection  and remote control through the S-Miles Cloud platform.
