The HMS-500-1T (MPN: HMS-500-1T) from [Hoymiles](https://www.offgridtec.com/marken/hoymiles.html) is the perfect micro inverter for your balcony power plant. The compact, lightweight device (weighing only 1.75 kg) is suitable for solar power production with an output of 400 to 670+ watts. Just like all other micro inverters from Hoymiles, it is suitable for long operating hours and a wide range of PV module applications, as its start voltage is very low (only 22V) and the DC input range of the operating voltage (16-60V) is extremely wide.

## Features

- Minimization of the effects of e.g. shading, dust, or aging through [MPPT](https://www.offgridtec.com/en/lexikon/mppt-technologie/ "MPPT")
- Optimal system reliability
- Peak efficiency of 96.5%
- 12-year product warranty – [information on the warranty conditions can be found here](https://www.offgridtec.com/media/product_attachements/Hoymiles_Garantiebedingungen.pdf)
- High level of safety thanks to integrated protective functions
- Easy installation
- Long service life
- Complies with EN 50549-1: 2019, VDE-AR-N 4105: 2018, VFR2019,


IEC/EN 62109-1/-2, IEC/EN 61000-6-1/-2/-3/-4, IEC/EN 61000-3-2/-3

  - In order to be able to change settings in addition to monitoring, a [DTU Pro-S](https://www.offgridtec.com/hoymiles-dtu-pro-s-wlan-datenuebertragungseinheit-fuer-hmt-hms.html) is required instead of the DTU Lite-S

## Optimal reliability

Micro inverter systems are inherently more reliable than conventional inverters. The decentralized design of a micro inverter system ensures that a fault does not cause the entire PV system to fail. Hoymiles microinverters are designed to operate at full power at ambient temperatures of up to +65°C (150°F). The microinverter housing is designed for outdoor installation and complies with protection class IP67.

## Simple installation

PV systems with Hoymiles microinverters are very easy to plan. There is no need to install cumbersome conventional inverters. Each microinverter can be quickly mounted on the PV mounting rack or directly under the PV module. The PV module is connected directly to the microinverter on site, eliminating the risk of people being exposed to dangerously high DC voltage.

- Recommended module power: 400 to 670+W

- Maximum input voltage: 65V
- Maximum input current: 16A
- Maximum input short-circuit current: 25A
- Rated output power: 500VA
- Rated output current: 2.17A
- Rated output voltage/range: 230V (180 - 275V)
- CEC peak efficiency: 96.5%
- Ambient temperature range: -40°C - +65°C
- Dimensions (WxHxD): 182 × 164 × 30 mm
- Weight: 1.75 kg
- Housing rating: IP67
