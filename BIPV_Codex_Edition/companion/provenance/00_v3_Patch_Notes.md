# iWin-Type BIPV Project Companion — v3 Patch Notes

## What changed

v3 adds a **vendor-data acquisition layer** on top of the v2 pre-design pack.

New files:
- `08_Vendor_Technical_Questionnaire.md`
- `09_Vendor_Response_Matrix.tsv`
- `10_Vendor_Evaluation_Rubric.md`
- `11_Vendor_Data_Request_Cover_Note.md`
- `12_Vendor_Response_Workbook.xlsx`

## Why this matters

The v2 pack was strong on architecture, standards, thermal framing, and commissioning structure, but it still depended on **vendor-specific closures** for:
- actual PV technology and ratings
- cavity/chamber and feedthrough details
- actuator/control architecture
- isolation and replacement boundary
- connector/cable/feedthrough definitions
- qualification basis for the offered build
- commissioning and warranty evidence

v3 turns those open items into a **controlled request-and-review workflow**.
