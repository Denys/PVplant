## BDM 300 Microinverter

**Qualified equipment**that meets the Hawaiian Electric TROV-2 and full frequency and travel voltage The**BDM 300** offers cable options including conventional trunkcable and daisy chain.**It is the thinnest microinverter in the world** at just 0.98” thick.

This model has a high continuous output power of up to **300w AC**, recommended for **photovoltaic modules** of 450W maximum and fully complies with the NEC 2014/2017 section 690. 12 requirements for fast shutdown. No additional equipment required.

[View datasheet](https://northernep.com/products/microinverters/bdm-300/#datos-tecnicos)

## International certificates

Globally certified to C-ETL-us, SAA, TUV, VDE-AR-N 4105, VDE 0126, G83 / 2, CEI 021, IEC61727, EN50438 Protection class IP66, IP67, NEMA 6 high efficiency with 95.5% maximum.

## Simple and practical design

Dimensions: 7.09'' x 7.32'' x  0.98'' weight of 3.3 lbs, Operating temperature of -40°F to +185°F. Natural convection cooling system.



## Simple installation

Very simple installation with built-in cables and connectors. Integrated monitoring and power line communication with integrated BDG-256 GFDI gateway. Integrated ground connection for easy installation.

## NEP Microinverters

Simple to install, the **Microinverter** is placed in the same structure as the **solar panels** and the cable connections are made through its own connectors.

High efficiency and excellent profit with **maximum conversion efficiency** of up to 95.5%. With **BDM 300** you take full advantage of the power of solar energy with MPPT technology.​
