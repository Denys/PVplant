Ampt’s patented features increase system ROI

Ampt String Optimizers are DC/DC converters with multiple features that lower the cost and improve the performance
of new and existing PV systems.

Dual MPPT per optimizer - Ampt String Optimizers
put maximum power point tracking on each of its two
input strings of PV modules. This mitigates or eliminates
mismatch losses to deliver more energy under changing
environmental and system conditions over the lifetime of
the power plant.

High Fixed-Voltage Bus (HFVB) – Ampt’s HFVB
technology enables the DC bus to operate at a constant
voltage that is higher than variable voltage systems.
The fixed voltage simplifies the connection and control
of system components tied to the DC bus. The higher
voltage delivers power at a lower current to reduce
component and system costs.
