### Product detailed description

## Key Features

- Module-level monitoring – full visibility into module- and system-level production
- Rapid shutdown – a UL Standards-certified component for photovoltaic rapid shutdown systems (PVRSS) worldwide
- Intelligent optimization – delivers the maximum energy from an array
- Simple, fast installation – snaps to a standard PV module frame or mounts to racking
- Monitoring, rapid shutdown, and remote troubleshooting with Tigo Access Point (TAP) and Cloud Connect Advanced (CCA)
- Works with any system – fully compatible with thousands of different [inverter](https://www.onsaplus.eu/glossary/inverter/) models from more than 50 inverter brands
- Suitable for up to 700W solar modules
- 25-year warranty
- Works wirelessly with the TAP & CCA
- Shade and age tolerance to maximize lifetime yield

Part number 462-01252-32 or 462-00252-32
